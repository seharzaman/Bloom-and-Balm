Hi, I'm Sabah Zaman

Melbourne-based, combination-skinned, and on a mission to figure out what actually works when you've moved from one side of the world to the other.

My skincare story

I grew up in Islamabad, Pakistan, where the sun is strong but the skincare culture is deeply personal. I had my routine. It worked. I didn't question it.

Then I moved to Melbourne, and December 2024 happened. I spent the month exploring Australia wearing the same Pakistani sunscreen I'd trusted for years — Factor 30, perfectly fine back home. It was not fine here.

By the end of that summer I had a sun tan deeper than anything I'd experienced in Pakistan. Melbourne's UV index hits 13 in summer. Islamabad peaks around 7. I started over from scratch.

Bloom & Balm is what I wished had existed when I arrived.

What I review

SPF, Korean beauty, moisturisers, and skincare routines. These are the categories that matter most when you're adjusting to Australian conditions — and the ones I wish I'd had honest, local guidance on from day one.

Why I test everything

Every product has spent real time on my skin. I buy with my own money. Affiliate links are disclosed in every post, and they never change what I write.
